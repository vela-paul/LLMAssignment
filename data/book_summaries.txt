## Title: 1984
A dystopian story about a totalitarian society controlled by constant surveillance and propaganda. Winston Smith secretly rebels in search of truth and freedom.
Themes: surveillance, oppression, freedom.

## Title: The Hobbit
Bilbo Baggins joins a company of dwarves to reclaim their homeland from the dragon Smaug. He discovers unexpected courage and friendship along the way.
Themes: adventure, friendship, courage.

## Title: The Lord of the Rings
Frodo Baggins must destroy the One Ring to save Middle-earth from the dark lord Sauron. A tale of fellowship and sacrifice.
Themes: heroism, fellowship, sacrifice.

## Title: Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone
A young wizard discovers his magical heritage and attends Hogwarts School, where he makes friends and faces a dark enemy.
Themes: magic, friendship, self-discovery.

## Title: To Kill a Mockingbird
Scout Finch grows up in a racially divided town as her father defends an innocent Black man. She learns about justice and empathy.
Themes: justice, racism, coming-of-age.

## Title: Pride and Prejudice
Elizabeth Bennet navigates manners and marriage in 19th-century England, sparring with the enigmatic Mr. Darcy.
Themes: love, class, personal growth.

## Title: The Catcher in the Rye
Holden Caulfield wanders New York City grappling with grief and the loss of innocence.
Themes: adolescence, alienation, identity.

## Title: The Chronicles of Narnia
Children stumble through a wardrobe into a magical world ruled by the wise lion Aslan and fight against the White Witch.
Themes: faith, adventure, redemption.

## Title: War and Peace
An epic tale following Russian families through love, war, and social change during Napoleon's invasion.
Themes: war, history, destiny.

## Title: The Book Thief
During WWII, young Liesel finds comfort in books while her foster family hides a Jewish man.
Themes: words, courage, war.
